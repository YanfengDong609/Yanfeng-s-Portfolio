In contemporary society, young people often have irregular eating and sleeping schedules. Losing weight has attracted a lot of people's attention.

Before I started this project, my grandpa passed away. Truly believing that “Memory is a way of holding on to the things you love, the things you are, the things you never want to lose,” I created this comic project to dedicate to the memory of my grandpa.
